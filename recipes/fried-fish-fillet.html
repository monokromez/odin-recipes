Fried Fish Fillet

Description

Fried Fish Fillet is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that features tender fish fillets coated in a crispy batter and deep-fried to perfection. It is a popular choice for seafood lovers and can be served with various sides.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/4 tsp of paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 450 g of fish fillets

Steps

  1. Mix flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in a deep plate.
  2. Coat the fish fillets in the flour mixture and shake off excess.
  3. Fry the fish fillets for 5 minutes until golden brown.
