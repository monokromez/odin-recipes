Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a popular dish in many parts of the world, known for its rich and flavorful sauce. It typically consists of chicken pieces cooked in a spiced sauce. This dish is perfect for a hearty meal and can be served with rice or bread.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chicken Broth
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Bayleaf
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Curry Powder
  • Fish Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the chicken.
  2. Sauté aromatics and curry powder.
  3. Simmer the chicken.
  4. Add the carrots, potatoes, coconut milk, and evaporated milk.
  5. Add the bell peppers.
