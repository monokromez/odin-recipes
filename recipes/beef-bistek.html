Beef Bistek

Description

Beef Bistek is a popular Filipino dish made with thin slices of beef cooked in a savory sauce. It is typically served with rice and is known for its tender texture and rich flavor.

Ingredients

  • Thinly-sliced Beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Calamansi
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef in soy sauce and calamansi, minced garlic, sugar, and ground black pepper
  2. Once marinated, fry the beef until cooked through, then set aside
  3. In the same pan, cook the onions until softened.
  4. Pour in the marinade and reduce slightly.
  5. Add the beef back in and toss to combine.
